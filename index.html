RC Plane of the Month

Aeroscout

The Aeroscout is a beginner-friendly trainer plane with a stable and durable design. With its forgiving flight characteristics and durable construction, it is an excellent choice for new pilots learning the basics of RC flying. It flies excellently and can do acrobatics well, making it a great choice for advanced pilots as well. Its propeller is placed behind the wings, so you'll never break the propeller even in the worst crash you can get into.

Additionally, the Aeroscout features tricycle landing gear, making takeoffs and landings smooth and easy. Its high-wing configuration provides extra stability, and the SAFE technology helps to fly the plane straight and prevent stalling, giving beginners a safer learning experience. With a lightweight yet durable foam airframe, it can withstand harsh crashes and rough landings while remaining easy to repair with a bit of foam safe glue.
